Best Practices

The Creator
Playbook.

A guide to thriving in the Xprex ecosystem. How to create, engage, and earn.

1. What should I create?

On Xprex, we don't chase "trends" that disappear in 24 hours. We value Legacy Content.

Ask yourself: "Will this video still add value to someone a month from now?"

  • Share Knowledge: Teach a skill, explain a concept, or break down industry insights.
  • Tell Stories: Share personal experiences that resonate with the Nigerian reality.
  • Show Process: Don't just show the result; show how you built it.
üí° Pro Tip: The algorithm rewards "Completion Rate." Create content that is compelling enough to watch until the very end.

2. How earning works

Unlike other platforms, you do not need to manually put your content behind a paywall.

Once you are accepted into the Partner Program, 100% of your videos become eligible to earn. You earn revenue whenever a Paying Member watches your content. The more time they spend with you, the more value you are providing, and the more you earn.

3. The Social Multiplier

Xprex is a community, not a broadcast tower. Growth here is reciprocal.

Engaging with other creators is not a distraction‚Äîit is a growth strategy. When you leave thoughtful comments and support other creators, you build visibility for your own profile.

Don't be a ghost. Build friendships. Collaborations and shout-outs within the ecosystem are the fastest way to grow your "Follow Signal."

4. Tracking your success

Transparency is our core value. You can track your performance in real-time.

To view your earnings:
Navigate to Profile > Creator Hub > Monetization.

This dashboard displays your accumulated earnings, current Partner status, and payout history. Payments are processed monthly for all accrued earnings over the threshold.
